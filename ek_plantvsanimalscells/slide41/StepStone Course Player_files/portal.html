Module Title: Cell types
Step Title: Cell Wall
Step Type : Basic
Step Text Content : Cell Wall
The cell wall is found in plants, algae, fungi and some bacteria. It is the tough/rigid outer covering that protects and provides the shape of the cell.
All plant cells have a plasma membrane and a cell wall.
